CRM SERVICES

Customer relationship management (CRM) software helps your business organize and optimize relationships with your existing and potential customers. Email marketing and CRM platforms can be used together to organize contacts, automate campaigns and store customer data.Many email marketing platforms either integrate with or have their own CRM built into their software. CRM software will work with email marketing by:

  1. Syncing lead and customer information with other systems
  2. Sending automated internal alerts for things like scheduled calls or sending follow-up emails
  3. Showing where an individual customer is in the sales funnel
  4. Tracking a customer's history with your business
  5. Identifying a potential customer within the funnel
  6. Providing detailed information that allows the company to create personalized email marketing campaigns
